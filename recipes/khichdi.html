Instant Pot Khichdi

Description

Khichdi is a simple yet famous South-Asian dish, which can be called simply, a rice and lentil porridge (salted and spiced)!


More about how to make it at home below! Eat it first before judging it!

Since there is no particular way to make Khichdi, we are providing with what we know about it


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup lentils (preferably moong dhal)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds(jeera)
  • 1/2 tablespoon ghee (or vegetable oil)
  • 5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Put the pot on stove and add ghee (or oil) in the pot
  2. When it heats up (indicated by chit-chit sounds), add cumin (jeera)
  3. Add five cup of water into the pot
  4. Add salt and black pepper into the pot next
  5. Wash the rice and lentils and then add the washed material into the pot
  6. When water starts boiling, turn the gas slow, and put a lid on the pot
  7. Wait for 10 minutes, and then check if water has been vaporized
  8. Simple yet delicious Khichdi is ready, enjoy!
