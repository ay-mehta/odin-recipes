Chai - Indian Milk Tea

Who does not like a cup of chai in the morning, with friends, maybe over a meeting, in rainy season, in winter season?

If you haven't tried it out, what are you waiting for? Get the FOMO and check out the below recipe!


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon chai-patti
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Add water in teapot and put it on stove
  2. Add crushed cardamom, sugar and chai-patti in teapot
  3. Then cover the teapot with a lid
  4. When water starts to boil, add milk to the teapot
  5. When milk and water mix together, and it starts boiling, turn down the flames for a few seconds
  6. Take the tea and strain it, pour it in a teacup of your choice, enjoy!
