Phulka Roti

Description

Phulka Roti is an extremely lightweight version of the flattened bread - a staple in many South-Asian regions, try it! It is extremely simple to make!


Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add salt to wheat flour in a bowl
  2. Slowly add water while mixing the flour with your hands
  3. Knead the dough till it comes in a smooth form
  4. Now cover the dough with a wet cloth for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes, knead the dough again for 2 minutes
  6. Now, handpluck dough balls the size of a lemon from the big dough monster
  7. Apply some dry wheat flour to the baby dough balls
  8. Flatten the baby dough ball using a rolling pin (belan)
  9. Put the flattened dough on frying pan
  10. When it gets heated up from one side, flip it
  11. Do the above again and press it lightly with the help of kitchen towel
  12. When it inflates, remove it from the frying pan
  13. You now have a Phulka Roti, Enjoy!
